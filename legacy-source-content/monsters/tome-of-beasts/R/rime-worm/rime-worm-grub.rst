gnash attack.

**Tendril.** *Melee Weapon Attack.* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage.

**Gnash.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 12 (2d8 + 3) slashing damage.
